Material Collection

Natural stone is never repeated — each piece has its own pattern, density and character. That is why every SNM FENIX product is unique and created not from a template, but for a specific space. We work with stone in a way that enhances architecture rather than competing with it. Practical. Exclusive. Durable.

Travertine

Warm texture and architectural character. Works perfectly in minimalist and Mediterranean interiors.

  • Advantages: natural texture, comfortable tone, organic pattern
  • Best for: vanities, wall cladding, countertops and panels
  • Important: porous stone — requires proper sealing

Onyx

Stone for accent solutions. Its translucency reveals a dramatic effect when backlit.

  • Advantages: depth of pattern, translucency, premium accent
  • Best for: sinks, panels, inserts and decorative zones
  • Important: requires careful maintenance and proper construction

Limestone

Calm uniform structure and soft tactile feel — pure architecture without visual noise.

  • Advantages: elegant matte surface, natural palette, versatility
  • Best for: wall panels, window sills, shelves and interior elements
  • Important: protection against chemicals and sealing required

Marble

Expressive veining and rich color palette. Perfect when aesthetics and uniqueness are essential.

  • Advantages: premium appearance, unique patterns, prestige
  • Best for: bathtubs, sinks, walls and bathroom countertops
  • Important: sensitive to acids — sealing and proper care required

Labradorite

Iridescent reflections create a strong premium accent, especially in dark interiors and lighting.

  • Advantages: color depth and reflections
  • Best for: countertops, panels, bars and accent details
  • Important: polishing reveals the iridescence

Granite

Maximum practicality and durability. Ideal for intensive use and commercial spaces.

  • Advantages: hardness, resistance to moisture and scratches
  • Best for: kitchen countertops, sinks and window sills
  • Important: one of the most durable natural stones

Sandstone

Natural earthy texture and soft aesthetics. Pairs well with wood, metal and warm lighting.

  • Advantages: expressive texture and natural appearance
  • Best for: cladding, décor and accent elements
  • Important: porous — sealing required
